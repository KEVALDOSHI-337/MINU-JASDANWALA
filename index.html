About

Minu Jasdanwala is a well-respected educator and passionate literary figure based in Rajkot, Gujarat. He is the founder of Kafka’s English Corner, guiding students in communication and personality development. He also runs "Minu’s Mehfil", an open mic poetry platform for budding poets and authors. His influence spans literature, education, and social work.

"Every student is a spark, and I just help them ignite." – Minu Jasdanwala
